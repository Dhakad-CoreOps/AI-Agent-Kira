# Business Travel Policy

This document governs all company-paid business travel. For anything not
covered here, contact HR before booking.

## 1. Approval

- All business travel must be approved in writing by your manager **before**
  any booking is made.
- International travel additionally requires approval from the department head.

## 2. Booking & Class of Travel

- Flights: economy class for all flights under 6 hours; premium economy is
  allowed for flights of 6 hours or longer.
- Trains: AC 2-tier or equivalent.
- Hotels: up to **₹6,000 per night** in metro cities, **₹4,000 per night**
  elsewhere. Book through the company travel portal where possible.

## 3. Daily Allowance (Per Diem)

- Domestic travel: **₹1,500 per day** for meals and incidentals.
- International travel: **USD 50 per day**.
- Per diem does not require receipts; everything above it does.

## 4. Reimbursement

- Submit travel expense claims on the HR portal within **10 working days** of
  returning from the trip, with receipts attached.
- Approved claims are paid out with the next salary cycle.

## 5. Not Covered

- Alcohol, minibar charges, and in-room entertainment are never reimbursed.
- Fines (traffic, parking) and personal side-trips are the employee's own cost.
