# Company Handbook

Welcome to the team! This handbook is the official reference for company
policies. If anything here is unclear, please contact HR directly.

## 1. Working Hours

- Standard working hours are **9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday** (IST).
- Employees may shift their start time by up to 1 hour with manager approval.
- Remote work is allowed up to **2 days per week**, coordinated with your team.

## 2. Leave Policy

- Every employee receives **24 days of paid leave** per calendar year.
- Unused paid leave carries over up to a maximum of **8 days** into the next year.
- **Sick leave**: 10 days per year, separate from paid leave. A medical
  certificate is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.
- Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least **3 working
  days in advance**, except for emergencies and sick leave.

## 3. Payroll & Reimbursements

- Salaries are credited on the **last working day of each month**.
- Work-related expenses (travel, client meetings, approved software) are
  reimbursed within **15 days** of submitting receipts on the HR portal.
- Internet reimbursement for remote-work days: up to **₹1,000 per month**.

## 4. Probation & Notice Period

- New employees serve a probation period of **3 months**.
- Notice period during probation: **15 days**. After confirmation: **60 days**.

## 5. Code of Conduct

- Treat all colleagues with respect. Harassment or discrimination of any kind
  results in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
- Company data and candidate information are confidential and must never be
  shared outside authorised systems.
- Conflicts of interest (e.g. outside employment with a competitor) must be
  disclosed to HR in writing.

## 6. IT & Security

- Use your company account for all work communication; enable two-factor
  authentication on all company services.
- Report lost devices or suspected security incidents to IT within **24 hours**.
